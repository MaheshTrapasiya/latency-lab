# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.
Examples of positive behavior include:

- Showing empathy and respect for different experiences and viewpoints.
- Giving constructive technical feedback and accepting it gracefully.
- Focusing discussion on what is best for users and the project.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and helping repair their impact.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks,
sexualized language or attention, threats, trolling, and publishing another
person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and
other contributions that violate these standards. They may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the contact
options on the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/MaheshTrapasiya).
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project or its community.
